About Me

I'm Geovani Debastiani, and I am 17 years old. I'm coffee lover living in Brazil - Santa Catarina.


In 2017 I started studying programming with Small Basic in a course for which I received a scholarship.


The course introduced me to the vast world of programming that I love! From that point on, I began self-studying at home, focusing on systems and RPA'S development with python.


In 2020 I got my fisrt job in my life as a Young Apprentice at karsten, where I worked alongside seamstress, It was an amazing expirence, and even though It was a simple job, I learned a lot with them!


In 2022, I started working in this big world of programming, still as a Young Apprentice, but this time at Teclógica, where I had a training for work with web development with Java, JSF, Primefaces e Postgresql. It was a excellent expirence, and It was during this time that I encountered my first challenges in my career, a moment I will never forget.


In July of 2023, while still in the same company, I had the opportunity to become a Junior Developer. One of my dreams has come true!! I continued working with the same stack but also began learning Kotlin to work on our mobile software.


Since 2022, I've been studying English, something I discovered I really enjoy. I'm learning to use it at work, and in the future, I intend to move to another country.
